Closer

We have our premium copy. Now, we need a blueprint. Before we touch a single color or image, we must map out the skeleton of Aisha's website so the user's eye flows effortlessly toward the "Book Now" button.

The Wireframe Rule

Building a website without a wireframe is like building a house without a floor plan. You will end up with doors on the ceiling. A Wireframe is just a simple map of where things go.

Every High-Income booking page follows the exact same 4-part anatomy:
1. The Hero Section: Big headline, stunning background image, primary Call-To-Action (CTA).
2. The Agitation/Trust Section: Where we paste the "Problem" copy we wrote in Phase 1.
3. The Solution/Services: The bullet points of what Aisha actually offers.
4. The Final Closer: A massive, undeniable CTA to book her calendar.
