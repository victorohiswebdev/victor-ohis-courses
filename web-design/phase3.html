Whitespace & Contrast

The quickest way to spot an amateur website is clutter. If a website looks cramped, the customer assumes the service is cheap. Luxury breathes. This is the power of Whitespace.

As we paste our AI copy into our structural boxes, we are going to use the Rule of Contrast. Dark backgrounds get light text. Light backgrounds get dark text. And we will leave massive padding (empty space) between every section so Aisha's bridal makeup photos can shine. Let the images do the talking, and the copy do the selling.
