The Reality Check:
The Live Simulation

Never, ever hand over a bot to a client without stress-testing it first. It is time to step into the shoes of the customer and watch Chioma's 24/7 Sales Rep close a deal in real-time.

The "Aha!" Moment

When you are selling this service, business owners will be skeptical. They will say, "Bots are annoying, my customers won't like it." The only way to overcome that objection is to show them a flawless simulation.

We are going to run a 3-Step Stress Test. We will test the Ad Trigger, we will test the Organic Menu, and most importantly, we will deliberately type nonsense to make sure our Fallback Rule catches the error smoothly.

The Execution

Testing the funnel using a secondary number.

The Testing Protocol

"Grab a second phone, or have a friend message your WhatsApp number. We are going to roleplay as a difficult customer and watch the AI handle the pressure flawlessly."

01. Test The Ad

Send the exact phrase: "I saw your ad on Instagram." Ensure the Bone Straight price script fires instantly.

02. Test The Menu

Send "Hello". Wait for the menu. Then reply strictly with "1" and ensure the correct product catalog drops.

03. Break It

Send something random like "Where is the shop?". Ensure the Fallback rule catches it and politely redirects them.
