Mastering
Character Consistency

The fastest way to ruin a motion graphic video is if your main character looks like a different person in every scene. We must teach the AI to lock in specific visual traits.

The Anchor Prompt

AI image generators suffer from "amnesia." Every time you hit generate, they start from scratch. To prevent this, Digital-Preneurs use an Anchor Prompt.

Instead of just saying "A girl studying," and then later saying "A girl winning an award," we must define the exact molecular makeup of the character and paste that definition into every single scene prompt. We define her hair, her skin tone, the style of animation, and the color palette. This forces the AI to keep her consistent.

Generating Fatima's 5 Scenes in Google AI Studio.

The Consistent Formula

[The Anchor]: "Flat 2D vector illustration, modern corporate art style. A 20-year-old Nigerian female engineering student with short natural hair, wearing a yellow safety helmet and a blue denim shirt."

+ [The Action]: "...sitting at a messy desk looking exhausted, alarm clock ringing."

01. The Anchor

Copy the Anchor text. This is the visual DNA of our "Engineering Girlie." You must paste this at the beginning of all 5 scene prompts.

02. The Action

Append the specific action for the scene. (e.g., Scene 1: exhausted desk. Scene 5: holding a trophy, smiling).

03. UI Assets

Generate secondary assets like the "APWEN Resource Hub Phone Mockup" and floating scholarship icons to layer over your character later.
