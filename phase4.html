The Power of Context

This is the secret to charging High-Income rates: Perceived Value. If you send a client a flat logo on a white background, their brain sees a graphic. If you send them that same logo printed on a physical product, their brain sees a million-dollar business.

Instead of searching Google for overused, generic mockup templates, we are going to command Leonardo.ai to generate a custom, hyper-realistic blank food box. Then, we will use Canva to map our logo onto it perfectly.

The Execution

Generating the blank asset in Leonardo.ai & printing it digitally in Canva.

The Mockup Prompt

"A blank, unbranded eco-friendly kraft paper food delivery box sitting on a rustic wooden table in a brightly lit modern kitchen. Hyper-realistic, 8k resolution, cinematic lighting. The front of the box must be completely blank, flat, and perfectly positioned for a logo mockup."

01.

Generate the blank box in Leonardo.ai. Look for a clean, flat surface facing the camera.

02.

Import into a 1920x1080 Canva Presentation file. Drop your transparent Phase 2 logo on top of the box.

03.

The Pro Trick: Lower the logo transparency to 85% so the texture of the cardboard shows through. It makes it look printed!
